Philosophy
01
The engine drives value.
Others take the spotlight.
I build the systems that make decisions possible — traceable, explainable and economically sound. The output speaks. I don't need to.
02
Complexity is the input.
Clarity is the product.
Multi-layer materials. Country-specific regulations. Imperfect data. These aren't obstacles — they're exactly where structured decision logic creates the most value.
03
Excel is not a decision engine.
It just pretends to be.
Manual evaluation leads to inconsistent decisions, invisible risk and dependency on individual knowledge. Structured engines don't forget. They don't have bad days.
04
Regulation is not a cost.
It's a decision variable.
PPWR, EPR and DPP are economic forces — not compliance checkboxes. The organisations that treat them as inputs to decision models will outperform those that don't.
05
If it lives only in someone's head,
it isn't a system.
Tasks, owners, timelines and status scattered across emails and memory is not project management — it's organised chaos. Dynamic tracking turns parallel workstreams into visible, actionable structure. Requested by colleagues. That's the only metric that matters.
06
What comes next?
That's the right question. If you think you know the answer — let's talk.
What I Build
PTDE
Active · POC
Packaging Tender Decision Engine — replaces Excel-based tender evaluation with structured, line-level scoring across commercial, technical and regulatory dimensions. Handles PPWR/EPR impact, multi-material complexity and spend-weighted supplier aggregation. Built in C# / Blazor.
→
VMI Systems
Deployed · Live
Four VMI technologies implemented across Lemvigh-Müller and Sanistål/Ahlsell — ESL, weight-based replenishment, vending automater and StockMaster. Started as a concept we pushed to market. Became a standard customers write into tenders. Live in production across Denmark.
→
Cost Intelligence
In Use
Cross-country pricing model tracking cost impact and % movement across 7 countries and 7 business units in real time. Replaces manual monthly reporting with structured, supplier-level visibility. Built for packaging category management.
→
Regulus
Vision · Roadmap
Modular decision platform for areas where regulation, data quality and economic outcomes are tightly connected. PTDE is the first module. Designed for extensibility across PPWR interpretation, EPR fee logic, DPP documentation and supplier risk — by country and domain.
